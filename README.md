# Privacy Policy for FocusFlow - Pomodoro Timer

**Last updated: June 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how FocusFlow ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. By using FocusFlow, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**FocusFlow does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.**

Specifically, we do **not** collect:

- Personal identification details (name, email, phone number)
- Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, device serial number)
- Location data (GPS, IP-based location)
- Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
- Crash reports or performance logs
- Payment or financial information
- Photos, media files, or document access
- Contacts, calendars, or messages

All data generated by the App — such as session history, timer statistics, achievements, and settings — is stored **exclusively on your device** in local memory. This data is never sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Since FocusFlow does not collect any personal information, there is no data usage to describe. All features of the App operate entirely offline and locally on your device.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- All user data (session records, statistics, achievements, preferences) is stored in the app's local memory on your device only.
- We do not use any cloud storage, remote databases, or external servers.
- Since no data leaves your device, the risk of data interception or unauthorized access during transmission is zero.
- We implement standard Android platform security to protect local data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. If you have concerns about the security of your data, you may clear the app data at any time through your device settings.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

FocusFlow does **not** integrate, communicate with, or send data to any third-party services, including but not limited to:

- Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Advertising networks (Google AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
- Social media SDKs (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
- Crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
- Cloud storage providers
- Payment processors

The App is fully functional without any network connection and does not require internet access to operate.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, rent, lease, trade, or otherwise distribute any user data to third parties — because we do not collect any data in the first place.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

FocusFlow is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children because we do not collect personal information from anyone. The App contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social features that would require personal data.

---

## 7. Permissions

FocusFlow requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|---|---|---|
| `WAKE_LOCK` | Keep the screen on during focus sessions (optional, user-controlled) | No |
| `VIBRATE` | Provide haptic feedback when a timer completes (optional, user-controlled) | No |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` (Android 13+) | Show timer status notifications (if notification feature is enabled) | No |

All permissions are optional and can be denied without affecting core timer functionality. The App will never request permissions beyond what is necessary for its stated features.

---

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

- All data is stored locally on your device and persists only as long as the App is installed.
- **To delete all your data**, simply uninstall the App from your device. This will permanently remove all session history, statistics, achievements, and settings.
- You can also clear app data through: **Settings > Apps > FocusFlow > Storage > Clear Data**
- Since no data is stored externally, uninstalling the App ensures complete and irreversible data deletion.

---

## 9. International Users

FocusFlow can be used from any location worldwide. Since we do not collect or process any personal data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. The App operates entirely on your local device regardless of your geographic location.
